Our Team

George Kenney

George Kenney is co-founder of Shepherd Ventures III. The fund invests in AI Tech and MedTech companies that have trustworthy management teams and have AI-based IP moats. George was also founder and Managing Director of Shepherd Ventures II, a Tech, SW and Life Science fund that invested in 18 companies. He has a strong technical background combined with extensive Wall Street and financial industry experience. George also founded five startups, four of which were successful. He has syndicated many debt and equity financings for a broad range of technology and financial companies. George currently runs Start-Up Lab and with 30+ certified graduates. He is also CEO of Black Swan Research, where he invented a "black swan flu shot" that protects stock portfolios from market meltdowns.

Before forming Shepherd Ventures, George was CTO and Partner at Nicholas-Applegate, a money management firm in San Diego, where he directed technology and operations to grow and sell the company. Prior to Nicholas-Applegate, George was a Managing Director at Kidder Peabody in New York City, becoming an expert in Investment Risk Management. Previously he held top positions at Swiss Bank, Salomon Brothers, American Stock Exchange, North American Philips and five start-ups. George holds numerous US and foreign patents.

George holds an MBA in Finance from Columbia, an MSEE from Stanford and a BSEE from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Additionally, he is a Graduate of Harvard’s Industrial Management Program. George was an elected Trustee of the San Diego Pension Board (SDCERS) with $8 billion in AUM. He has served as a Trustee for the San Diego Museum of Art and as pro-bono Chairman of the Museum's Investment Committee.  He has also served on the Board of Governors of the National Association of Small Business Investment Companies and is a seasoned board member of start-up companies.
